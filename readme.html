Mining Fracture Analyser

Introduction

The Mining Fracture Analyser is a powerful, real-time calculation tool designed specifically for cooperative mining crews in Star Citizen, such as those using multiple MOLEs, Prospectors, or Golems.

This application simplifies complex in-game mechanics by calculating your crew's Total Combined Effective Laser Power (MW) and comparing it against a targeted rock's mass, effective resistance, and instability. Use it to pre-determine if your team has enough power to successfully fracture a difficult asteroid or planet-side rock before committing time and resources.

How to Use the App

The analyser is designed for quick input based on your in-game ship loadouts and the rock data displayed on your scanner.

Step 1: Assemble Your Mining Team

  1. Use the "1. Assemble Your Mining Team" dropdown to select a ship (e.g., Argo MOLE or MISC Prospector).
  2. Click Add Ship. The app will automatically add the corresponding number of laser heads (e.g., 3 heads for a MOLE, 1 for a Prospector) to the configuration area.

Step 2: Configure Loadouts

For every active Laser Head added:

  1. Laser Head (Base Power): Select the actual mining laser head installed (e.g., Lancet MH2, Helix I).
  2. Modules: Select up to three Power Modules (e.g., Surge, Rieger-C3). Only modules with a power multiplier (>1.0) will increase your effective fracture power.

Step 3: Apply Gadget

  1. In the "2. Select Gadget" section, choose the resistance-reducing gadget your crew plans to use on the rock (e.g., Sabir). This applies a single, non-stacking resistance reduction to the target rock.

Step 4: Input Rock Parameters

  1. Enter the rock's Base Resistance (%) and Rock Instability (%) values exactly as shown on your ship's mining scanner.
  2. Enter the Target Rock Mass (kg) for the specific rock you intend to fracture.

Step 5: Calculate

  1. Click the "Calculate Combined Fracture Limit" button.

What Do You Get From It?

The results section provides the critical metrics needed for a successful fracture:

  1. Effective Resistance: The final resistance of the rock after your chosen gadget has been applied.
  2. Total Combined Effective Laser Power (MW): The sum of the raw power from all active laser heads, multiplied by the effects of all chosen modules. This is your crew's true fracture strength.
  3. Max Theoretical Breakable Mass Limit (kg): The largest rock mass your current team configuration can theoretically crack, given the rock's Effective Resistance.
  4. VERDICT: A clear indicator of FRACTURE SUCCESSFUL or FRACTURE FAILED.
  5. Upgrade Required (If Failed): If the fracture fails, the app provides the exact Required Effective Power (MW) you need to safely crack the rock and offers recommendations for the maximum-power module loadouts.
